## ğŸ“ Customization

### Personal Information

Update the following files with your information:

1. **components/Hero.tsx** - Your name and introduction
2. **components/About.tsx** - Your background and journey
3. **components/Skills.tsx** - Your technical skills
4. **components/Projects.tsx** - Your projects
5. **components/Contact.tsx** - Your contact information
6. **components/Navbar.tsx** - Social media links
7. **app/layout.tsx** - Meta information

### Add Your Projects

In `components/Projects.tsx`, update the `projects` array with your actual projects.

### Update Skills

In `components/Skills.tsx`, customize the `skillCategories` array with your skills.
